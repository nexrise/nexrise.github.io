THE FUTURE OF TEACHING & LEARNING IN EDUCATION

How to integrate technology with pedagogy to maximise learning outcomes

Innovation with technology is creating new opportunities to enhance the student learning experience and improve educational outcomes. However, rather than jumping on the latest tech tool or trend, if technology is going to bean asset to academics and learners, it can be integrated through a design-led approach.

In this interactive session, SME’s will provide practical insight into “pedagogy before technology” approach for integrating technology into teaching and learning in an authentic way. SME’s will empower participants with the tools to innovate with technology to improve student motivation and learning outcomes.

Attend and learn how to:

  • Develop the skills and capabilities in staff and students to support the effective use of technology in learning.
  • Apply a design-led approach: identifying the problem, understanding how technology can help and identifying the solutions.
  • Ensuring authentic integration of technology into assessment.
  • Develop creative approaches to enhance student engagement in learning.

Know how:

  • Navigating Change and Transformation in Teaching and Learning
  • Integrating Technology into Pedagogy to Enhance Student Learning
  • Leveraging Learning Design and Curriculum Reform to Deliver Better Student Outcomes
  • Embedding Employability into Learning and Teaching
  • Pedagogy before Technology - Mapping Learning Outcomes with Technology
  • Driving Transformation in Teaching and Learning across the School, College and University
  • Applying Learning from MOOC MicroMasters to Enhance Learning Design
  • Enhancing the Employability of Students with Life Skill
