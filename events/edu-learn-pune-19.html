Introduction

EDU-LEARN’19 is one of the largest national education conferences for lecturers, researchers, technologists and professionals from the educational sector. It will become a reference event where more than 250 attendees and 50 exhibitors will get together to present their projects and share their knowledge on teaching and learning methodologies and educational innovations. The edition of EDU-LEARN’19 is sure to be among the most successful education conferences in India.

EDU-LEARN’19 is more than a conference. It is an ideal platform for national and international strategic networking, the best place to present your innovations and projects about education and technology.
