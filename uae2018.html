The Future Management, Leadership and Knowledge Summit - Dubai 2018

InterContinental Dubai - United Arab Emirates
3rd May 2018

NexRise Events provides the attendees with a broad ideas, knowledge and survey of competing views on what Knowledge Management is. Attendees will beneﬁt an executable ideology for understanding and designing the Knowledge strategies. Knowledge Management has long been a staple activity in the ﬁeld of Business. We look for easily trans-forming tacit experiences into explicit knowledge, and then disseminating that knowledge through-out the chain of command. Looking globally, the best Knowledge Management practices are those that Develop Talent, Growth and Innovation. Increasingly, therefore, the emphasis is on Executing Talent, Reuse Existing Skills and Expertise, Necessary for Achieving Organizational Acceleration and Business Goals. The impact of mega events on Knowledge Management, will further accelerate demand for a more professional approach to people strategies and policies.
